Our Story

These are my 90-year-old grandparents. Alice, my grandma, has onset dementia and a pacemaker and takes numerous medication multiple times a day. Sometimes, she does not remember to take her pills, know which one to take, and easily mistakes one of those little white pills for another. While I’m away at college and not there to organize her pills, she can easily over or underdose. Pill Beacon is my solution to help my family, and the many around the world, provide the stress-free care our elders deserve.

Caregiving is hard enough. We’re here for you.
